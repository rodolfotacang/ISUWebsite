COLLEGE GOAL

To provide broad general education in the Criminology that is geared towards the integral development of the human beings;
To strengthen the program of instruction designed to enhance creativeness and competitiveness towards the upliftment of the quality of life;
To develop students’ capabilities necessary to nurture leadership potential for his/her role in environmental protection and preservation;
To develop a creative and analytical mind in search of the truth, the good and the beautiful.
To stimulate development of the individuals’ ideas, attitudes, and the habits desirable for the improvement of the quality of his family, community, and country.



What Can I Do with a Criminology Degree?

The Bachelor of Science in Criminology or Criminal Justice is a four-year degree program intended for individuals who wish to have a career in the fields of law enforcement, security administration, crime detection, and prevention of correctional administration. The program teaches students the various theories, policies, practices, and laws associated with criminal behavior and the methods applied to manage deviant activities.
